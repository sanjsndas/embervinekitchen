Privacy policy

We treat your data like a good sourdough starter — with care, respect, and never shared without permission.

Your privacy matters at Embervine

Last updated: June 2026. This policy explains how Embervine Kitchen Foundry collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or interact with us.

📌 In short:

We collect only what you give us (name, email, messages). We never sell or rent your data. We use it to reply to you, send you the newsletter you signed up for, and improve our recipes & content. That’s it.

1. What we collect

  • Contact form: name, email address, subject, and message.
  • Newsletter sign-up: email address.
  • Cookies: we use minimal cookies for site functionality and analytics (see our Cookies Policy).
  • Usage data: aggregated, anonymized data about page visits to help us understand what content you love.

2. How we use your information

  • Reply to your questions, recipe pitches, or collaboration requests.
  • Send you the weekly “Fire & Flour” newsletter (only if you opted in).
  • Improve our website and content based on what our community engages with.
  • Comply with legal obligations — we never share data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data storage & security

We store your data securely on encrypted servers. Access is limited to core team members. We retain contact form submissions for up to 2 years for customer service purposes, unless you ask us to delete them sooner.

4. Your rights

  • Access: you can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: update or correct your information at any time.
  • Erasure: ask us to delete your personal data — we’ll do it promptly.
  • Opt-out: unsubscribe from our newsletter with one click (link in every email).

To exercise any of these rights, just drop us a line at privacy@embervinekitchen.com.

5. Third-party links

Our website may link to external sites (e.g., ingredient suppliers, book recommendations). We are not responsible for their privacy practices, so we encourage you to read their policies.

6. Children’s privacy

Embervine Kitchen is for everyone, but we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you’re a parent or guardian and believe we have information about your child, please contact us.

7. Updates to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We’ll post any changes here and, if significant, we’ll notify you via email or a banner on our site.
