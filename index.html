    Frequently asked questions

    What is the best flour for sourdough?

    High-protein bread flour (12–14%) gives the best gluten development, but blending with whole grain adds depth.

    How do I fix a broken emulsion?

    Start with a small amount of warm water or egg yolk, then whisk the broken sauce in slowly — it will re-emulsify.

    Why does my caramel crystallize?

    Usually from undissolved sugar crystals on the pan sides. Brush down with water or use a wet brush during cooking.
