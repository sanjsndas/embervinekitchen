Cookies policy

We use cookies like a pinch of salt — just enough to enhance the experience, never to overpower.

How we use cookies at Embervine

Last updated: June 2026. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

🍪 In short:

We use only essential and analytics cookies to keep the site running smoothly and to understand which recipes you love. We never use tracking cookies for advertising or sell your data.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, improve site performance, and gather anonymous usage data.

2. Types of cookies we use

CookiePurposeDuration
session_idEssential: keeps you logged in / remembers form submissionssession
preferencesRemembers your preferred view (e.g., dark/light mode)1 year
_ga (analytics)Anonymized traffic data via Google Analytics2 years
_gid (analytics)Helps us understand which pages are most popular24 hours

Note: we do not use any third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

3. Why we use cookies

  • Essential: to keep the site functional (e.g., session management).
  • Analytics: to see which recipes and articles our community enjoys most — this helps us create better content.
  • Preferences: to remember your settings so you don’t have to set them each visit.

4. Managing your cookie preferences

You can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block all cookies, but please note that some parts of our site may not work properly without them.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data.
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data.
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions.

5. Third-party cookies

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. Google may set its own cookies, but we have configured them to be privacy-friendly (IP anonymization enabled). We do not use any other third-party cookies.

6. Updates to this policy

We may update this cookies policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or regulation. We’ll post any changes here and, if significant, we’ll notify you via a banner on our site.
